Operation Data Fortress: An Overview

Global Synergy Solutions has identified and is actively managing a cybersecurity incident. This report provides transparent details on the event, our response, and our commitment to security.

Who is GSS?

Global Synergy Solutions is a leading multinational corporation providing innovative services across the finance, healthcare, and education sectors, committed to excellence and security.

What Happened?

We identified a sophisticated cybersecurity incident that resulted in unauthorized access to certain sensitive data stored on our AWS cloud platform.

How Did It Happen?

Our investigation indicates the incident originated from a targeted spear-phishing campaign, which allowed attackers to deploy malware and exfiltrate data.
